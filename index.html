The Founder

Meet Malini Sridhar

Malini Sridhar is a 20-year airline industry professional, trained facilitator, and entrepreneur based in Kuwait. Having spent two decades navigating one of the world's most demanding industries, she understands the power of communication, confidence, and presence.

She began learning Bharatanatyam in her late 30s, not because it was easy, but because she believes growth has no age limit. She performed her Arangetram (solo debut recital) at 45. That belief became a mission.

The Confident Voice Academy is a transformation programme for young people aged 10 to 16 who have everything to say but are hesitant to speak up. In a world where children are digitally fluent but publicly silent, she guides them to introduce themselves with presence, hold a room, tell their story, and speak with the kind of confidence that opens doors.

Her students leave not just less shy. They leave transformed.
